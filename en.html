System of a Down

System of a Down is an American alternative metal band known for its heavy sound, political lyrics, and unique style.

About the band

The group became famous with albums such as Toxicity, Mezmerize, and Hypnotize.

Why they are famous

  • Distinctive musical style
  • Lyrics about social and political issues
  • Energetic and unforgettable live performances
